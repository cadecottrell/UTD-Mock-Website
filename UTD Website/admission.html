Information on admission criteria for UTD

Each applicant is reviewed from the following factors:

Overall grades in academic coursework and trends in achievement
This includes awards for academic exellence

Strength of high school curriculum, and level of rigor
AP, IB, dual credit courses

High school rank and GPA
Includes Texas Top 10%, GPA is based off a 4.0 scale

SAT or ACT scores
The mid 50% SAT Range is 1210-1430 and the mid 50% ACT range is 25-31

Preperation for and ability to be successful in intended major
High school courses related to the major is a good topic to bring up.

Information provided in the resume, optional essay and/or optional letters of recommendation
Letters of recommendation are suggested, but are limited to three
